## What I Have Planned (Conceptually)
BIE is envisioned as a **standalone executable application** that helps centralize, reason about, and manage security posture and compliance evidence across **Windows and Linux environments**.

## Core Capability Areas (High-Level)
- Assessment & evidence ingestion  
- Explainable risk and exposure analysis  
- STIG review and remediation automation  
- Continuous audit policy verification  
- Enclave-level compliance and posture visibility  

## Design Philosophy
- Standalone, no mandatory SIEM dependency  
- Evidence-first, deterministic, and explainable logic  
- Optimized for reducing repetitive compliance effort  
- Focused on audit defensibility and operational clarity
